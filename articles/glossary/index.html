Bortle Scale

Nine level scale that qualifies night sky's brightness. It is used to measure light pollution. More info is available on Wikipedia

Calibration frames

In astrophotography, frames used to calibrate image in preprocessing. These are: bias, dark and flat frames.

LP

Light pollution - it is skyglow created mainly by artificial light (in urban areas). LP reduces contrast, making visual observations hard, or even not possible. It is measured in Bortle Scale or mag/arcsec2.
